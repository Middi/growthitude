Create an Engaging Work Culture







You’ll learn what it takes to create an engaging work culture like they have in companies such as Google and IDEO. You’ll also learn what you need to shift within your own company culture for the lasting success of the business. We will also make sure everyone within your company understands the company vision, the values, the mission and the strategy and is given a clear role in them.

PURPOSE The workshop intends to be an open invitation to all to engage and involve themselves actively into embracing and enriching the above and find ways in which to bring them into life in their daily jobs.

INTENDED AUDIENCE

All employees within your company.

TAKE AWAYS VVMS paper visuals; Mood Meter® and The Compass™

POSSIBLE REINFORCEMENT ACTIONS Digital visuals with CEO communication. Town Hall Morning Espresso.

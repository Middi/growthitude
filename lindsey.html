Our podcast with Lindsey Agness

26 December, 2016



Our guest in this podcast is someone who is an amazing mentor. I met her when I was still in Google and needed tools that could help me to help our clients to create 10x transformations. Her mentorship has made me understand what is needed to create the results I want and she has been a role model for me when becoming an entrepreneur.


Lindsey Agness spent 11 years as a change management consultant and behavioural change specialist at PricewaterhouseCoopers before setting up The Change Corporation in 2005. At The Change Corporation they specialise in the ‘people side of change’. This primarily focuses on organisation development projects including culture and behavioural change often designing and delivering large, customised development programmes. They also offer change management consultancy services, advising organisations on how to engage and motivate staff during periods of transformational change.


Lindsey is also an author of four published books on both organisational and personal change and a Master Trainer of Neuro Linguistic Programming.
